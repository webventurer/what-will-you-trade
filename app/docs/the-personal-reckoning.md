- Would I trade time I don't enjoy for money I need?

## The confrontation

Here's the harsh truth: **you are already answering these questions, every day.** Whether you've thought about it or not.

- If you work a job you hate, you've decided your time is worth trading for stability
- If you sell a product you know is bullshit, you've decided deception is worth it
- If you chase shortcuts and lose everything, you've decided gambling feels easier than building
- If you give your body, your voice, or your attention for cash, you've decided that's your best currency

And maybe that's fine. Maybe that's your choice. **But own it.**

Because pretending you're "above it all" while grinding away at something meaningless isn't noble. It's cowardice.
